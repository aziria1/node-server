Social Work and Coding

Below you will find a handfull of my work that I've accomplished over the past few years.
Each project has detail descriptions, dates and technology used.

Imagine Santurce Web Site

December 2015

This website was created for the Imagine Santurce Initiative. It was built using Wordpress and Buddypress to ensure easy customization from anyone in the initiative.We are currently working on integrating and training the whole network of participants in the possible uses for the page.

Caminantes o Mirones

November 2015

In this project I used the apps developed by arcgis to develop a community storytelling digital map for PRpinnaple Tours. The map was developed from the recopilation of stories told by community members of the Rio Piedras barrio.

Santurce Community Asset Mapping

February - October 2015

Using the the participatory toolkit developed by Los Angeles Healthy City, I developed a participatoy community asset mapping plan for Santurce, that later on transform into a multisectorial initiative.The University of Puerto Rico, along side San Juan Ultra, the Forest Service, the San Juan Municipality and multiple Santurce communities decided to join the mapping initiative. I led the preparation, grant writing and plan development for an EPA Grant that will allow us to cover cost for the initiative that starts in January 2014.
